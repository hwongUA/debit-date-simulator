## Design Context

### Users
Internal operations and fundraising staff using a browser-based planning tool to understand how debit timing and pack dispatch timing line up for supporters over time.

### Brand Personality
Clear, dependable, operational. The interface should feel calm and trustworthy rather than playful or consumer-marketing driven.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-mode planning board with editorial typography, paper-toned surfaces, and strong schedule markers. The visual system should feel intentional and analytical, not generic SaaS.

### Design Principles
- Make date relationships legible before making them decorative.
- Keep assumptions visible so planners can validate business rules quickly.
- Use strong contrast between debit and fulfilment markers.
- Preserve desktop clarity while keeping mobile access workable.
